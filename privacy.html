Privacy Policy

1. Who We Are

AccountingMage is operated by James Heuze trading as AccountingMage. We provide branded tax estimator widgets for UK bookkeepers and accountants.

We are the data controller for the personal data described in this policy. If you have any questions about how we handle your data, contact us at accountingmage@outlook.com.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect only what we need to deliver our service. This includes:

Data When collected Why
Name Contact form / purchase To address you correctly
Email address Contact form / purchase To communicate with you about your subscription
Firm name Contact form / purchase To brand your widget correctly
Website platform Contact form To provide appropriate integration guidance
Payment information Purchase Processed directly by Stripe — we never see or store your card details

We do not collect any data from visitors who use the embedded widget on your website. The widget runs entirely client-side and sends no data to our servers.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data only to:

  • Respond to your enquiry and set up your widget
  • Manage your subscription and process payments
  • Notify you of annual rate updates or changes to our service
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not use your data for marketing to third parties, profiling, or any automated decision-making.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

Under UK GDPR, we process your personal data on the following bases:

  • Contract performance — processing your name, email, and firm name is necessary to deliver your subscription
  • Legitimate interests — to communicate with you about service updates and renewals
  • Legal obligation — where required to comply with applicable law

5. Who We Share Data With

We share your data only with the following third-party processors, and only to the extent necessary to deliver the service:

  • Stripe — payment processing. Your payment data is handled directly by Stripe under their own Privacy Policy. We do not store card details.
  • Netlify — our hosting provider and form processor. Enquiry form submissions are received via Netlify. See Netlify's Privacy Policy.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any other third parties.

6. How Long We Keep Your Data

We retain your data for as long as your subscription is active. If you cancel, we keep your basic account details for up to 12 months for administrative and legal purposes, after which it is deleted.

Enquiry form submissions from non-customers are deleted within 6 months if no subscription is taken out.

7. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction — ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Deletion — ask us to delete your data (subject to any legal obligations to retain it)
  • Restriction — ask us to limit how we use your data
  • Portability — request your data in a portable format
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interests

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at accountingmage@outlook.com. We will respond within 30 days.

If you believe we have not handled your data correctly, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO).

8. Cookies & Tracking

The AccountingMage website does not use tracking cookies, analytics scripts, or advertising pixels. We do not track visitor behaviour on this site.

Netlify may set functional cookies necessary for form submission and site delivery. These are strictly necessary and do not require consent.

9. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal data. All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Payments are handled entirely by Stripe, which is PCI DSS compliant. We do not store payment card information.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify active subscribers by email. The latest version will always be available at this URL.
